Sainidhi

I'm a bridal makeup artist based in Chennai. My journey into makeup started from Visual Communication and a career in media, working as an Assistant Director at Star Vijay and later in advertising. But makeup had quietly been my passion for years, so I decided to pursue it professionally and trained at Chic Studios in New York.

After returning to Chennai, I worked on fashion and brand shoots before naturally finding my way into bridal makeup. For the past 10 years, I've had the privilege of being part of brides' most special moments. I love the intimacy of wedding mornings, the emotions, the excitement, and the trust that comes with what I do.

My style is soft, elegant, and skin-focused. I believe makeup should enhance you, not change you. So you still look like yourself, just more confident and radiant.

With over 10 years of experience in the bridal industry, Sainidhi brings a perfect blend of skill, creativity, and calm to every bride she works with. Professionally trained in cut, colour, and hairstyling at Bounce Academy, she developed a strong technical foundation before discovering her true calling in bridal hairstyling.

Her deep interest in hair science and structure allows her to design styles that are not only elegant and long-lasting but thoughtfully tailored to each bride's hair type, comfort, and personality. Known for her gentle presence and attention to detail, she creates looks that feel effortless yet refined.

We began our bridal journey together with our very first bride and have been partners ever since. The energy we bring to every wedding morning is something our brides often talk about: fun, easy, and calming. We make sure every bride feels relaxed, confident, and completely taken care of.
