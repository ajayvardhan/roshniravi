About

Hi, I'm Roshni Ravi

I'm a bridal makeup artist based in Chennai. My journey into makeup wasn't a straight one. I studied Visual Communication and began my career working as an Assistant Director at Star Vijay, and later as an editor in advertising. While I loved being part of the creative industry, I always felt drawn to something more personal and hands-on.

Makeup had quietly been that interest for years. So I decided to pursue it professionally and trained at Chic Studios in New York. That experience gave me the foundation, confidence, and clarity to turn my passion into a career.

After returning to Chennai, I worked on fashion and brand shoots before naturally finding my way into bridal makeup. That's where I truly found my place.

For the past 10 years, I've had the privilege of working closely with brides, being part of their most special moments. I love the intimacy of wedding mornings, the emotions, the excitement, and the trust that comes with what I do.

My style is soft, elegant, and skin-focused. I believe makeup should enhance you, not change you. So you still look like yourself, just more confident and radiant.

If you're looking for someone calm, detail-oriented, and genuinely invested in making you feel your best, I'd love to be a part of your day.
